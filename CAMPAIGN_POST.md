# UniSync - Campaign Main Points

## 🎯 One-Line Pitch
**UniSync** — Find your perfect university duo based on your qualities, niche, and ambitions.

---

## 📌 Main Points for Campaign Post

### 1. Problem Statement
- University life can be overwhelming — finding the right study partner, project teammate, or friend shouldn't be.
- Students struggle to connect with others who share their goals, study habits, and interests.

### 2. What is UniSync?
- A smart matching platform built exclusively for university students.
- Connect with peers who match your **qualities**, **niche**, and **ambitions**.
- Whether you need a study buddy, a project partner, or a genuine friend — UniSync brings you together.

### 3. Key Features
| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| 🔍 Smart Matching | Find students based on qualities, niche & study habits |
| ✅ Verified Community | Email OTP verification ensures a safe, trusted environment |
| 💬 In-App Chat | Connect and chat directly within the platform |
| ❤️ Meaningful Connections | Build real relationships with like-minded students |
| 🛡️ Admin Approval | Every profile is reviewed before joining for quality |

### 4. How It Works (3 Simple Steps)
1. **Create Your Profile** — Sign up with university details, add your photo, share your qualities
2. **Find Your Match** — Browse students and send connection requests
3. **Start Connecting** — Once accepted, chat and build your duo relationship!

### 5. Who Built It?
- Built by **Shoaib Jan**, a Software Engineering student at Emerson University, Multan
- Session 2024-28
- Created to help students find the perfect study partner and build meaningful campus connections

### 6. Why Choose UniSync?
- ✅ Made **by students, for students**
- ✅ No random connections — every match is intentional
- ✅ Safe & verified community
- ✅ Completely free to use
